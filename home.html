Jesse Carl

SONGWRITER | PRODUCER | MIX ENGINEER 

Jesse Carl is a multi-instrumentalist, producer, and mix engineer based in Milwaukee, WI. At the young age of 8 years old, he had the honor of sharing the stage with artists Natalie Cole and Dolly Parton. At his current age of 25, he has not slowed his roll. Jesse has performed alongside globally recognized artists such as Vinyl Theatre, Jesse McCartney, NeverShoutNever, and Newsboys, to name a few.

Jesse is a talented and creative person who is quick on his feet and a pleasure to work with. He isn't afraid of working hard and always has new ideas to throw into the mix. I appreciate his collaborative spirit and his ability to play all genres of music on the fly. Wish I could hire him for every gig!

Colleen Schmitt

Music Director at Hyde Park UMC
