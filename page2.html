Jesse Carl

Jesse Carl is a multi-instrumentalist, producer, and mix engineer based in Milwaukee, WI. At the young age of 8 years old, he had the honor of sharing the stage with artists Natalie Cole and Dolly Parton. At his current age of 25, he has not slowed his roll. Jesse has performed alongside globally recognized artists such as Vinyl Theatre, Jesse McCartney, NeverShoutNever, and Lizzo, to name a few.
